The Hyperplanes and Lines

A hyperplane \(H\) in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) is the set of all vectors \( v = (x_1,...,x_n) \) such that \(a_1x_1 + ... + a_nx_n = c \) for scalars \(a_1,...,a_n\) and \(c\).
